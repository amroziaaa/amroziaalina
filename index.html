About Me

I am a Media Engineering (B.Eng.) student at TH Nuremberg, combining technical expertise with creative vision in Video and Audio Technology and Post-Production.

My studies deepen my understanding of video/audio technology, lighting, media formats, compression, programming, and media informatics, giving me a strong technical foundation.

I have practical experience in cinematography, editing, color grading, sound design, motion graphics, and digital storytelling for reels, events, and social media. My work blends technical precision with a clear visual style.

This portfolio reflects my ability to carry productions from concept to final delivery, working independently, reliably, and creatively.
